Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Snake Retro ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application.

Summary: Snake Retro does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your privacy is fully protected.

Information Collection

Snake Retro does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No personal data is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No third-party analytics services
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No contact information

Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • High scores and game progress
  • Game settings (sound preferences)
  • Daily challenge records

This data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to any external servers.

Third-Party Services

Snake Retro does not use any third-party services that collect data, including:

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media integrations
  • No crash reporting services

Advertisements

Snake Retro does not display any advertisements and does not integrate with any advertising networks.

In-App Purchases

Snake Retro does not offer any in-app purchases. The app is completely free with all features available.

Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13, our app is safe for users of all ages. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

Permissions

Snake Retro does not require any special permissions. The app functions entirely offline and does not access:

  • Camera or microphone
  • Contacts or calendar
  • Location services
  • Storage (beyond app-specific storage)
  • Network or internet (not required)

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data breach or unauthorized access to your information through our app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. Your game data is stored locally on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the app.
